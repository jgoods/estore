This website showcases the works of Jude Goodpaster, which include custom, handcrafted furniture and home decor. Blacksmith/carpenter Jude Goodpaster does not consider himself an artist, yet his dedication and passion for his craft prove otherwise. Energetic and young-at-heart, Jude captures his exuberance for life and weaves it into his creations. Besides sculpting furniture, Jude also enjoys designing and building custom homes.
